About Me

I'm a game programmer from Toronto, Ontario. Making games is what I love to do, especially when I'm making off beat, or just plain weird ones. I get excited by trying new (usually absurd) things, and a lot of what I make reflects that. I do a lot of work with Unity3D, it's great and makes my life a lot easier, and outside of my personal projects, I work as a professional Unity3D and iOS developer.
